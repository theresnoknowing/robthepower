Based somewhere between the hum of city streets and the quiet of open space, Rob Power makes downtempo electronic music for late nights and long journeys. Drawing on texture, atmosphere and slow-burning rhythm, each track is built to feel like a place rather than just a song. The Lost Mine is his debut EP — four tracks recorded across 2024, released December of that year.
